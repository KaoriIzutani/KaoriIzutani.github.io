About me

A graduate of the Tech Academy, my love for web design began during my college years. In my sophomore year at Pacific University, I took a Computer Science 101 course. That course introduced me to the language where webpages could come to life.

Later on, I realized that the HTML 2.0 I learned at Pacific University was no longer going to cut it in today's high tech society. It was time to update my skillset and learn more cutting edge techniques when it came to coding.

My specialty lies in HTML and CSS. I have a working knowledge of GitHub, Bootstrap, jQuery, React and JavaScript. When not coding, I like to play mobile games (current game I'm playing: Fire Emblem Heroes) and doting on my cats, Beansprout and Grunty.
